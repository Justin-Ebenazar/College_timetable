Course Syllabus

Month 1: Fundamentals

  • Introduction to piano keyboard
  • Proper hand positioning
  • Basic music notation
  • Simple melodies with right hand

Month 2: Building Skills

  • Left hand coordination
  • Basic chords
  • Rhythm patterns
  • Simple songs with both hands

Month 3: Putting It All Together

  • More complex chords
  • Introduction to scales
  • Playing with expression
  • Performance preparation
